# Governance

SNEPPX-Algo is a BDFL (Benevolent Dictator For Life) project.

## BDFL

Ammar [SNEPPX] <algoSNEPPX@gmail.com>

## Powers

The BDFL has absolute and final authority over all aspects of the project:

- All technical decisions
- Repository access and commit rights
- Release authority and scheduling
- Security vulnerability response
- Patch acceptance and rejection
- Feature decisions and prioritization
- Architecture changes
- Documentation scope and content
- Community conduct enforcement
- License and legal matters
- Governance amendments

## Succession

Succession is undefined. The BDFL serves for life. If the BDFL becomes unable
to serve, the project may be archived or successor appointed at the BDFL's
sole discretion prior to incapacitation.

## Governance Model

- No council
- No voting
- No term limits
- No committees
- No appeals process

## Patch Acceptance

All patches are reviewed by the BDFL. No delegation of review authority.
Response time: 7 days typical, 30 days maximum.

## Conflict Resolution

The BDFL decides. Final. No appeals.

## Amendments

This governance document may only be amended by the BDFL.
