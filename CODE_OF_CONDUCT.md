# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Technical merit above all
- Direct communication
- No politics
- No discrimination
- No harassment
- No personal attacks
- Respectful disagreement focused on technical substance

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment of any kind
- Discriminatory language or actions
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

The BDFL (Ammar [SNEPPX]) has the sole authority to enforce this Code of Conduct.
All decisions are final. No appeals.

## Reporting

Report violations to algoSNEPPX@gmail.com.

All reports will be reviewed by the BDFL and will remain confidential.

## Consequences

1. Warning — verbal or written notice
2. Temporary ban — 30-day suspension from participation
3. Permanent ban — indefinite removal from all project spaces

The BDFL determines which consequence applies based on the severity and
context of the violation.
